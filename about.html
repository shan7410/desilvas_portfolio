My Story

I am Shan De Silva born on 1974 October 08. I received Primary and Secondary education at Sacred Heart Catholic School Sri Lanka. I have two daughters and one son. My Hobbies are reading history and Sci-Fi books, Hiking and listening to Classic Music’s. During my off I mostly spend my time with blissful kids and wife.

I am gifted and talented in fixing and working with mechanical stuff. At the age of 14, I designed and assembled my own two-seater motorbike. Bike parts are collected from various garages where parts has been unusable and trashed. I collected the parts and fix them, and modify. As the result, I had a full working Motor Bike which can ride 80KM per hour. That was the first Motor Bike written as a “Made in Ceylon”.

My Future dream after retired to run my own Pineapple and Banana Farm in Philippines. I used to watch my grandfather cultivating various vegetables on backyard. Most green vegetables he eats them without cooking plug on the spot as is fresh. My grandfather told me eats vegetables before its dies. He lived healthy long life.

Thank you for reading about me.
